FAQ

What clients ask before engaging.

What exactly does LA Global do?

We're a global consultancy operating across three practice areas — Media, Technology, and Capital. That can mean strategic advisory, hands-on production and execution, fractional executive leadership (like a fractional CDO or CBO), or access to growth capital through our investor network. Most engagements blend two or more of these.

Do you invest your own capital, or just make introductions?

We don't deploy capital directly. We advise on capital strategy, prepare you to raise, and make warm, qualified introductions into our network of family offices, angel investors, and institutional VC partners — the people actually writing the checks. Our role is to get you in the right room with the right story, not to be the investor ourselves.

What stage and raise size do you typically work with?

We're primarily focused on seed through Series A companies, with typical raises facilitated in the $250K–$1M range per relationship across our partner network. We'll always tell you directly if a company is outside our typical fit.

What's a fractional CDO or CBO, and how does that work?

A fractional executive is a senior leader — Chief Digital Officer or Chief Business Officer — who works with your company part-time rather than full-time, reporting directly to founders or the board. It's a way to get senior leadership without committing to a full-time executive hire.

How much does an engagement cost?

It depends on scope — advisory retainers, fractional leadership, and project-based production work are all priced differently. After an initial consultation, we send a transparent, custom proposal. No hidden fees.

How long does a typical engagement last?

Project-based work often runs 6–12 weeks. Advisory retainers and fractional leadership placements are typically structured in 3 to 12-month terms, renewed based on need rather than locked into long contracts by default.

Do you work with companies outside the US?

Yes. Our practice areas and capital network are built for clients operating across multiple markets, and our partner network spans North America, Europe, and the Middle East.

What happens after the discovery call if we're not a fit?

We'll tell you directly, and where possible point you toward a better-suited resource. We'd rather say no early than start an engagement that isn't right for either side.

How do I get started?

Book a free 30-minute strategy call. We'll talk through where the business is today and whether LA Global is the right partner — directly, with no obligation either way.

Have a question we didn't cover?

Reach out directly — we respond to every inquiry.

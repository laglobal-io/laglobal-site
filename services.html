SERVICES

Media and technology strategy — executed, and funded when needed.

Our core practice is business strategy consulting for media and technology companies. Capital access supports that work; it isn't a separate business line.

CORE PRACTICEMEDIA STRATEGY

Content, audience & distribution strategy

We build the editorial and production systems that turn raw content into a durable, monetizable audience — across owned channels, partnerships, and emerging platforms. Engagements range from a focused content audit to ongoing fractional oversight of a content operation.

  • Content strategy & production planning
  • Social and platform growth strategy
  • Influencer, creator & community partnerships
  • Brand and editorial positioning
  • Distribution & syndication partnerships
  • Monetization strategy: sponsorship, licensing & advertising
CORE PRACTICETECHNOLOGY STRATEGY

Product, infrastructure, AI & fractional leadership

We help technology and technology-enabled companies build the operating infrastructure to scale — including AI-enabled product strategy — and, where useful, sit inside the company as fractional executive leadership carrying real operating responsibility.

  • Digital infrastructure & operations strategy
  • Product, AI & go-to-market strategy
  • Fractional Chief Digital Officer (CDO)
  • Fractional Chief Business Officer (CBO)
  • Systems, tooling & process buildout
  • Technical due diligence support for fundraising
SUPPORTING PRACTICECAPITAL ACCESS

Capital strategy & investor access, when the plan calls for it

We advise on capital strategy and open doors into our network of family offices, angel investors, and institutional VC partners. LA Global doesn't deploy its own capital — our role is to get you raise-ready and into the right rooms, from readiness through close.

  • Capital strategy & fundraise readiness
  • Investor materials & data room preparation
  • Warm introductions to family offices & angels
  • Institutional VC partner introductions
  • Introductions for co-investment & syndicate opportunities
  • Cap table & term sheet advisory
ENGAGEMENT MODELS

Four ways to work with us.

Engagements are scoped, not templated — but most fall into one of four models. We'll recommend the right one on the discovery call.

01

Advisory Retainer

BEST FOR: ONGOING STRATEGIC GUIDANCE

Monthly retainer for strategic advisory across one or more practice areas — regular working sessions, not a one-time deliverable.

02

Project-Based Engagement

BEST FOR: A DEFINED DELIVERABLE

Fixed-scope work with a clear deliverable and timeline — a content system rebuild, a fundraise readiness sprint, a go-to-market plan.

03

Fractional Leadership

BEST FOR: AN OPERATING LEADERSHIP GAP

A fractional CDO or CBO embeds with your team on a defined weekly or monthly cadence, carrying real operating responsibility.

04

Capital Engagement

BEST FOR: AN UPCOMING RAISE

Capital strategy, readiness, and warm introductions to investors in our network — structured around your timeline to close.

FRACTIONAL LEADERSHIP

Senior leadership, without the full-time hire.

Our fractional executives embed directly with your team — typically a few days a week or a defined number of hours a month — to lead a function until you're ready for a full-time hire, or indefinitely.

FRACTIONAL

Chief Digital Officer (CDO)

Owns digital strategy, infrastructure, and execution — from platform architecture to digital growth operations — reporting directly to founders or the board.

  • Digital roadmap ownership and prioritization
  • Vendor and technical team oversight
  • AI and automation strategy
FRACTIONAL

Chief Business Officer (CBO)

Owns partnerships, business development, and revenue strategy — building the commercial relationships and deal pipeline a growth-stage company needs.

  • Partnership sourcing and negotiation
  • Revenue operations and pipeline strategy
  • Board and investor reporting support
CAPITAL STRUCTURES

Matching the capital to the stage.

We primarily work with seed and Series A companies, with typical check sizes in the $250K–$1M range per relationship across our family office, angel, and institutional VC network. Structure depends on stage, sector, and what the business actually needs:

Priced Equity RoundsSEED · SERIES A

Traditional priced equity financing, with LA Global advising on terms, cap table strategy, and investor selection.

SAFEs & Convertible NotesPRE-SEED · SEED

Faster, lower-friction structures for early-stage companies not yet ready to set a priced valuation.

Co-InvestmentSEED · SERIES A

We coordinate rounds where multiple investors from our network invest alongside one another, aligning incentives across the table — capitalized by our partners, not LA Global.

Angel SyndicationPRE-SEED · SEED

Pooling individual angel checks into a single coordinated line on the cap table.

Bridge & Extension RoundsSEED · SERIES A

Short-term capital to extend runway between larger priced rounds, often structured as notes or SAFEs.

Revenue-Based FinancingSEED+, REVENUE-GENERATING

Non-dilutive capital repaid as a percentage of revenue — an option for companies that don't want to give up equity.

Advisory EquityALL STAGES

Equity granted in exchange for ongoing strategic guidance or board/advisor involvement, in lieu of or alongside cash fees.

Board & Observer SeatsSEED · SERIES A

Formal governance involvement for investors and strategic partners who want a continued hand in company direction.

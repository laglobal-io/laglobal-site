ABOUT LA GLOBAL

Built to be one accountable partner, not three disconnected vendors.

LA Global was founded on a simple observation: companies don't fail from a lack of strategy — they fail from the gap between strategy, execution, and capital. We exist to close that gap.

Our Story

From media consulting to a full-stack global advisory.

LA Global started in media — helping broadcast networks, creators, and entertainment brands build digital strategy and production systems that could compete at a global scale. The early work was tactical: rebuild a content calendar, fix a distribution strategy, professionalize a creator's back office.

That work surfaced a pattern. The clients who grew fastest weren't just executing media strategy well — they were also getting their capital structure right and filling real leadership gaps at the same time. A media plan without the right operating leadership stalled. A technology roadmap without capital behind it stayed a roadmap. The constraint was never just one discipline; it was the seams between them.

Today, LA Global operates as a global consultancy across three disciplines — Media, Technology, and Capital — serving operating companies, founders, family offices, and investors who need a partner that can advise, execute, and help fund the plan, not just write it.

Methodology

The LA Global Method.

Every engagement — regardless of practice area or sector — runs through the same four phases. It's a deliberately simple structure, built to keep strategy and execution from drifting apart.

01
Diagnose

Understand the business as it actually runs

We map how the business actually operates — revenue drivers, content systems, technical infrastructure, cap table, and where leadership bandwidth is thin — rather than relying on the org chart or the pitch deck version of the story.

  • Operating & revenue audit across relevant practice areas
  • Identification of the binding constraint actually limiting growth
02
Design

Build a plan scoped to reality

We translate the diagnosis into a specific plan — which practice areas are engaged, what gets built or fixed first, and what capital strategy supports it — sized to the company's actual stage and resources.

  • Prioritized roadmap across Media, Technology, and Capital
  • Transparent proposal — no hidden fees, no open-ended retainers
03
Embed

Execute inside the business, not beside it

Our team — whether advisory or fractional leadership — works inside the company against the plan, with regular reporting and direct access to senior advisors throughout, not just at kickoff and renewal.

04
Compound

Leave systems that keep working

Where capital is part of the plan, we support the raise alongside the operating work already underway. Either way, the goal is systems, partnerships, and capital structure that keep compounding after the engagement ends.

How We Work

Principles, not just process.

01

Operator-first

We staff engagements with people who have built and run businesses, not generalist consultants reading from a framework. Advice is grounded in operating reality.

02

One accountable team

Strategy, execution, and capital introductions come from the same firm. No handoffs between a strategy shop, a production vendor, and a fundraising consultant.

03

Global by default

Our network spans family offices, angel investors, and institutional partners across multiple regions — engagements are built for companies operating beyond a single market.

04

Right-sized capital

We don't force every client toward a mega-round. We match the capital strategy — equity, notes, co-investment, or none at all — to what the business actually needs.

05

Direct, not diplomatic

If we're not the right partner for a given problem, we say so on the discovery call — not after a paid retainer has started.

06

Senior throughout

The advisors who scope the engagement stay involved through delivery. Work doesn't get handed off to a more junior bench once the contract is signed.
